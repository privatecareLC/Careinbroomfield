Services

What I Offer

I assist with activities of daily living so your loved one can remain comfortable, safe, and dignified at home. Every schedule and service is custom-tailored to fit your family's needs.

Reminders and careful oversight to keep medications on schedule — so nothing gets missed and your loved one stays safe and on track every day.

Nutritious, home-cooked meal preparation tailored to dietary preferences and needs, along with light kitchen cleanup so the space stays tidy and safe.

Maintaining a tidy, comfortable home environment — laundry, light tidying, vacuuming, and general upkeep so your loved one can enjoy a safe, organized space.

Reliable transportation to medical appointments, specialist visits, grocery trips, and everyday errands — so your family member stays connected and independent.

Compassionate, dignified assistance with bathing, showers, grooming, and dressing — always delivered with respect, patience, and sensitivity.

Genuine presence and companionship — conversation, shared activities, walks, or simply being there. Meaningful connection makes every day brighter.

Comfortable and happy around dogs — your four-legged family member is always welcome. No need to arrange alternative care or worry about your pet.

Flexible schedules built entirely around your family's needs — a few hours a week or a more consistent routine. No rigid packages, no one-size-fits-all plans.
